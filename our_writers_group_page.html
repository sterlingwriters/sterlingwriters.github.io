About Our Writers Group

We are a friendly "Creative Writing Group"

 - Open to anyone with an interest and a desire for writing.
 - Open to all skill levels (published, unpublished, want to be published,
    or just for fun!)
 - Open to all genres (Fiction, Non-Fiction, Science Fiction, Poetry,
   Personal Essay, Memoir, Children's, Short Story).
 - Weekly meetings are organized and moderated.
